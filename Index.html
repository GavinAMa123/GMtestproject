What is The Elite?


The Elite is a swartwatch and armband designed to push your limits and make you train longer, better, and smarter. These two wearables track your activities and collects info from your performance on sports. The Elite then takes that info and creates graphs, charts, and stats that would later be uploaded onto the Elite interface.The Elite gives its user choice, letting them control who can access the data. The smart wearables have been designed by a team of scientists to allow ultimate comfort while you train and perform, enabling you to look, play, and feel your best on and off the field, track pitch, or court. technology, used only by the Elite and its smart wearables.
